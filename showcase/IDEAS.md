# Showcase Ideas for vivid-onnx

Future showcase projects that demonstrate the full creative potential of this module.

## Proposed Projects

### Motion Capture Studio
**Status:** Proposed
**Complexity:** Complex
**Description:** Full-body motion capture with skeleton recording and playback.
**Key Features:**
- Multi-person pose tracking
- Motion recording to JSON/BVH
- Skeleton-driven 3D character animation

### Face Filter App
**Status:** Proposed
**Complexity:** Medium
**Description:** Real-time face filters with landmark tracking for AR-style effects.
**Key Features:**
- Face mesh overlay
- Expression detection (smile, blink, etc.)
- Multiple filter presets

### Hand Gesture Controller
**Status:** Proposed
**Complexity:** Medium
**Description:** Hand tracking for gesture-based parameter control.
**Key Features:**
- Pinch-to-adjust gesture for sliders
- Swipe gestures for preset switching
- Two-hand coordination modes
